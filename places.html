Hungary - Lake Balaton

Lake Balaton and its region is a major holiday destination with beaches, volcanic hills, wineries and lively coastal towns.There is plenty of accommodation to choose from, depending on whether you are looking to stay in a hotel with wellness facilities or staying in a private, self-contained apartment.

Balatonfoldvar

Balatonfoldvar opened a dog-friendly beach this summer, allowing you to enjoy the cooling down in the water with your dog on a hot summer day. Szandra Haz, a self-contained apartment house with garden and swimming pool is a short drive from the dog-friendly beach.

Book now

Fonyod

Fonyod also offers a dog-friendly beach, where you can swim with your dog and enjoy the sunshine. Tappancs (Paws) apartment house is within a short walking distance, and needless to say, dogs are welcome.

Book now

Keszthely

While there is no dog-friendly beach in Keszthely, Abbazia Club Hotel offers dog-friendly accommodation with wellness facilities. Dog-supervising services are also available if you wish to visit tourist attractions, such as the Festetics Palace or the Marzipan Museum, where dogs are not allowed - what a shame!

Book now

Spain - Costa Maresme & Costa Brava

One of the most beautiful coastal areas in Europe with spectacular award-winning beaches.

Bell Soll Campsite

Right in the seafront of Pineda de Mar, 49 km from Barcelona, Bell Soll Campsite is set just few minutes’ walk away from a sandy pet-friendly beach open all year around and without time restrictions. The site has an outdoor pool, a playground for kids and communal barbecues. Just off site in Pineda de Mar you can find a wide variety of restaurants, tapas bars and natural parks to visit.

Book now

Hotel Subur Maritim

Hotel Subur Maritim is a 4-star pet-friendly hotel situated in the charming town of Sitges. The accommodation features a large sea-view terrace, air conditioning in all rooms and an outdoor swimming pool. The hotel is 5.9 Km from Cala Vallcarca, one of the most popular pet-friendly beaches in the province of Barcelona. It is surrounded by natural parks and is open all year around and without time restrictions.

Book now

Lyon Hotel

Lyon hotel is a bright and cozy option located in Barcelona just a 3-minutes walk from the tube station Barceloneta. Ideal if you want to move around fast with your dog in Barcelona. Next to the hotel there is a park called Parc de la Barceloneta which has a doggy play area.

Book now

France - Paris

Paris, the City of Light, evokes romantic emotions in anyone who has ever visited. There is plenty to do in Paris to satisfy pet lovers. Is one of the best destinations in Europe with more than 1,000 pet friendly establishments, hotels and appartments.

Hôtel de Crillon

It is a historic luxury hotel. Located in 8th arrondissement, it is in the center of center in Paris. If you are staying in one of their pet friendly rooms while traveling with dogs, your pooch might even be treated better than you.

Book now

Actuel dogs

Although it is located in Vincennes, right outside of Paris, it is one of the most popular pet friendly destination for dogs because the service they provide will surprise you and your pooch.They offer dogs to swim in the pool, massages, walk in the woods nearby and even training!

Book now

The Peninsula Paris

The Peninsula Paris is located near Avenue Champs-Élysées. When you are traveling with dogs and they will be greeted with special welcome basket full of dog food, water, toy and a name tag. Also the best part is, the suites with private garden and terrace is available for you and pooch to relax together better.
