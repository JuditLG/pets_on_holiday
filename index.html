Dogs on Holiday

Spain - Costa Maresme & Costa Brava

One of the most beautiful coastal areas in Europe with spectacular award-winning beaches.

Bell Soll Campsite

Right in the seafront of Pineda de Mar, 49 km from Barcelona, Bell Soll Campsite is set just few minutes’ walk away from a sandy pet-friendly beach open all year around and without time restrictions. The site has an outdoor pool, a playground for kids and communal barbecues. Just off site in Pineda de Mar you can find a wide variety of restaurants, tapas bars and natural parks to visit.

Hotel Subur Maritim

Hotel Subur Maritim is a 4-star pet-friendly hotel situated in the charming town of Sitges. The accommodation features a large sea-view terrace, air conditioning in all rooms and an outdoor swimming pool. The hotel is 5.9 Km from Cala Vallcarca, one of the most popular pet-friendly beaches in the province of Barcelona. It is surrounded by natural parks and is open all year around and without time restrictions.
